About
Celphish Finance is a community-driven project that enables multi-chain liquidity sourcing and trading from various liquidity sources, such as DEXs that operate on several blockchain networks. This will allow users to trade and swap tokens at the lowest prices at the time. Our goal is to make crypto holders and enthusiasts perform crypto activities with their smartphones without any barriers.
